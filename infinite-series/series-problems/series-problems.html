Series problems!!!!

Which of the following series converge, and which diverge? Why? For those that converge, can you determine what they converge to? Don’t just give an answer—write out an actual argument, with your thinking and reasoning!

  1. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \left(\frac{1}{5}\right)^n\)
  2. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=2}^{n=\infty} \left(\frac{1}{5}\right)^n\)
  3. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \frac{1}{5^n}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \frac{1}{2^{n+3}}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \frac{2^{n+3}}{3^n}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \frac{3^{n-1}}{4^{3n+1}}\)
  7. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} (-1)^n\left(\frac{1}{5}\right)^n\)
  8. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\right)^n\)
  9. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \ln(n)\)
  10. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \frac{1}{n}\)
  11. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=20}^{n=\infty} \frac{1}{n}\)
  12. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \frac{1}{n\cdot2^n}\)
  13. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \ln(1/n)\)
  14. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} (-1)^{n+1}\frac{3}{2^n}\)
  15. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \left(\sqrt{2}\right)^n\)
  16. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \cos(n\pi)\)
  17. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty}\frac{\cos(n\pi)}{5^n}\)
  18. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} e^{-n}\)
  19. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} e^{-2n}\)
  20. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{k=\infty} \left( \frac{5}{2} \right)^{-k}\)
  21. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{k=\infty} \frac{1}{k^2}\)
  22. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{k=\infty} \frac{2}{k^4}\)
  23. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{k=\infty} \frac{\ln k}{k^2}\)
  24. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty}\frac{n^2+1}{n}\)
  25. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty}(-1)^{n+1}n\)
  26. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \frac{2}{10^n}\)
  27. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} \frac{2^n-1}{3^n}\)
  28. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \frac{n!}{1000^n}\)
  29. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} x^n\)
  30. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} (-1)^nx^n\)
  31. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{n=\infty} (-1)^nx^{2n}\)
  32. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{k=\infty} \frac{\ln \left(\sqrt{k}\right)}{k^2}\)
  33. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{k=\infty} \frac{1}{2+3^{-k}}\)
  34. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{k=\infty} \frac{1}{2+3^{k}}\)
  35. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \ln\left(\frac{n}{n+1}\right)\)
  36. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{n+1}} \right)\)
  37. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \left(\frac{1}{2^{1/n}} - \frac{1}{2^{1/(n+1)}} \right)\)
  38. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \left( \frac{1}{\ln(n+2)} - \frac{1}{\ln(n+1)} \right)\)
  39. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \left( \frac{1}{n^2+n} \right)\)
  40. \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{n=\infty} \left( \frac{1}{n} - \frac{1}{n+1} \right)\)
  41. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{k=\infty} \frac{k}{k^2+1}\)
  42. \(\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{k=\infty} \frac{1}{3k+2}\)
